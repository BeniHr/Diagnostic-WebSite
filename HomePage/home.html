Let us help you make your car purchases more secure

Getting a car from a used seller can be hard. With fake ads, hidden faults, and untruthful claims about vehicles at an all-time high, how can you trust that your new vehicle investment won’t incur expensive repairs and it works as advertised?

That’s where InspectWise is here to make your car purchase more secure.

One comprehensive & clear report

Our reports are comprehensive and intuitive, providing you with highlights and micro-details for the full picture. With this information, you can use it as leverage to negotiate a better price for your vehicle. Each report also has ongoing support from our experts to guide you through every step of your car inspection journey.

We take car inspection photos from all angles

Our inspectors are fully trained to spot hidden defects that car dealers often try to evade, and ensure you conduct a thorough top-down inspection so you are aware of any potential damage or defects in your vehicle.

We’ll be your car inspector and purchase “concierge”

We understand how inconvenient it can be to visit multiple car dealers in person (hope they're honest). That's why we take the load off your shoulders and handle the entire vehicle viewing and buying process for you, so you can relax in the comfort of your own home and be notified when your new car is ready (including vehicle transport).

Why should you chose us?

Your ideal car is not always near you
Can't find the perfect car? Perfect specs? The perfect color? It may not be available locally. As our partner, we open up the European market for you. We offer a simple, convenient and secure way to purchase a vehicle remotely, even from another country. We have agents all over Europe. You can find the car of your dreams in the comfort of your home.


You don’t know the condition of the vehicle
How do you determine the condition of the vehicle, if everything is in order or if it has been damaged and repaired? Even the most honest car dealer may not have the time to thoroughly inspect every car. You can send an InspectWise agent to the seller's location. Our reports are "super cool", thorough and easy to read. Any defects found will help negotiate the right price. You can avoid post-sale consumer disputes because you can have peace of mind knowing what you've purchased.


It can be hard to protect yourself against fraud
With two cars being stolen in Europe every minute, knowing your desired car is not stolen or cloned can be challenging. Questions such as, “Does the seller have the right to sell this car?” or “Have these documents been fabricated?” are legitimate because professional criminals are more skilled now than ever. With online being their favourite place to scam victims, you could be one of their next targets.
